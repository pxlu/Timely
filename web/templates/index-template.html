Timely

Timely is a student-led project based at the University of Toronto St. George campus. Our mission is to reduce wait times to provide timely access to professional psychiatric and psychological intervention for post-secondary students in Canada.

Learn More

Our Model

A Vulnerable Demographic

University-aged individuals are a growing part of the demographic that is particularly vulnerable to mental health issues, with almost a third of undergraduates reporting symptoms of elevated distress.

Severe Consequences

In large part due to mental illnesses, suicide is the 2nd leading cause of death among 15-24 year olds, accounting for 24% of all deaths in this age group. Over 80% of post-secondary students who commit suicide had never participated in counselling services on campus.

Economic Costs

Mental illnesses can signal a life-long struggle for students with social and productivity costs as consequences, and in turn contribute to long-term burdens to the health care system. The current economic burden of mental illnesses in Canada is $51 billion per year.
