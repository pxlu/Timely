Achieve Mental Wellness

Online tools to connect students and care providers, in order to build a complete care pathway for mental health.

Our Model

Comprehensive Tools

We offer comprehensive and up-to-date tools for screening, resource aggregation and treatment planning to help connect students with care providers for mental health care. Our solution works to streamline and supplement professional treatment in every step along the way.

Professional Partnerships

We work closely with various mental health care providers, establishing a framework of partnerships to guide individuals towards the best treatment paths possible. We aim to give students the flexibility and professionalism that they expect in their treatment journey.

Responsive Feedback

Our solution provides immediate and detailed usage feedback for students and care providers alike, allowing both parties to respond to changes and new information throughout the treatment path to better adapt to incoming situations.

Find Out More

Student-Centered Model

Students use our system free-of-charge to help kickstart and monitor their journey towards mental wellness, in collaboration with care professionals at every step.

Find Out How

Tight Professional Workflow

Care providers use our system to track, recommend, and guide students along their journey, with a myriad amount of data to gain a better understanding on which direction to proceed next.

Elastic Care Pathway

Both students and care providers alike can visualize the treatment path through our elastic care pathway generation tool.
